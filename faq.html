Frequently Asked Questions

What age can my child start martial arts?

We welcome children as young as 4 years old in our Little Dragons program. Classes are designed to be fun, safe, and developmentally appropriate.

Do I need prior experience to join?

No experience is needed! Our classes are beginner-friendly and our instructors will guide you at your own pace.

What styles of martial arts do you teach?

We offer a blended approach rooted in Jeet Kune Do, with elements of boxing, kickboxing, Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, and self-defense training.

Do I need to be in shape to start?

Nope! Many students start with no prior fitness background. Martial arts will help you build strength, flexibility, and endurance over time.

What should I wear to my first class?

Comfortable workout clothes like a t-shirt and athletic shorts or sweatpants. No shoes are needed—just bring water and a positive attitude!

Do you offer private or onsite sessions?

Yes! We offer private one-on-one training and onsite programs for schools, nonprofits, and organizations. Visit our Onsite or Private Training pages for details.

How do I get started?

Just contact us or visit the studio! Your first class is free—come try it out!
