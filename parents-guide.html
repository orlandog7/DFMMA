🧭 Parent’s Guide to Dragon Fist MMA

Everything you need to know before your child’s first class.

✅ What to Bring

  • Comfortable clothes: T-shirt and shorts or leggings are perfect. Uniforms are not required for trial classes.
  • Water bottle: We encourage students to stay hydrated.
  • No shoes on the mat: Martial arts is practiced barefoot for safety and hygiene.

⏰ Arrival & Pick-up

  • Please arrive 5–10 minutes early to give your child time to settle in and warm up.
  • Parents are welcome to stay and observe — quietly and respectfully.
  • Be prompt for pick-up. Classes end on time.

🧒 First Class Expectations

Trial classes are beginner-friendly with no pressure or contact. Instructors focus on structure, listening skills, basic movement, and fun. Students are grouped by age and taught at a pace that suits their level.

💬 Communication

Please let us know if your child has any injuries, behavioral concerns, or learning preferences. We're happy to accommodate. Feel free to chat with instructors before or after class, or contact us during business hours.

🧠 Our Teaching Philosophy

We believe martial arts is about more than punches and kicks. Your child will learn:

  • ✔️ Respect for themselves and others
  • ✔️ Focus and impulse control
  • ✔️ Confidence through progress and praise
  • ✔️ Resilience by overcoming challenges in a safe environment

🧾 Trial Classes, Membership & Safety

  • First class is always free. Please call ahead to reserve a spot.
  • No long-term contracts or high-pressure sales tactics.
  • We take safety seriously: clean mats, age-appropriate drills, and a respectful, supervised space.

🙌 Let’s Get Started

If you haven’t already, visit our Contact Page to book your free trial or ask any questions. We can’t wait to meet you and your child!
