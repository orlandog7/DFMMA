Little Dragons (Ages 4–6)

Our Little Dragons program is the perfect introduction to martial arts for children ages 4 to 6. These classes are full of movement, imagination, and fun—while quietly building the foundational skills of focus, respect, and coordination.

What Your Child Will Learn

  • Basic martial arts stances and movements
  • Listening, following instructions, and teamwork
  • Improved coordination, balance, and body control
  • Respect for themselves and others
  • Confidence through positive reinforcement

Every class is guided by caring instructors who know how to engage young minds while keeping safety and fun top of mind. It’s a joyful, empowering start to a lifelong journey of confidence and growth.
