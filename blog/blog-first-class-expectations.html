What to Expect at Your First Class

Starting something new can be intimidating — especially martial arts. Whether you’re an adult stepping onto the mat for the first time or a parent bringing your child, this post will walk you through exactly what to expect at your first class with Dragon Fist MMA.

1. No Gear Needed

For your first class, just wear comfortable workout clothes — t-shirt and shorts are perfect. We train barefoot on clean mats, so no shoes are necessary. If you decide to join, we’ll talk about uniforms and optional gear later.

2. Warm Welcome

You’ll be greeted by one of our instructors (usually Rob or Matt) and introduced to the group. If you're a parent, you're welcome to observe from the sidelines or ask questions before or after class.

3. A Guided Experience

We won’t throw you into sparring! First classes focus on movement, footwork, and simple techniques. You’ll go at your own pace and get one-on-one attention where needed. The vibe is friendly, respectful, and focused.

4. Kids Thrive with Structure

Our youth classes are structured and positive. Expect your child to participate in warm-ups, practice drills, and partner exercises — all geared toward building confidence, listening skills, and focus.

5. Everyone Sweats

Whether you're 7 or 70, martial arts is a full-body workout. Expect to move, stretch, balance, and engage muscles you forgot you had — but always at your own level.

6. You’ll Leave Smiling

Most first-timers walk off the mat feeling energized, proud, and a little sweaty. The most common feedback we hear? “That was way more fun than I expected.”

Still unsure? Reach out with any questions or just schedule your free trial class. We’ll take it from there.
