Private Training

Our Private Training program offers one-on-one martial arts coaching tailored to your goals, pace, and experience level. Whether you're a beginner, returning student, or someone seeking focused technical refinement, these personalized sessions are designed to help you grow confidently and efficiently.

Why Choose Private Training?

  • Individualized instruction aligned with your goals
  • Flexible scheduling that fits your lifestyle
  • Ideal for adults, teens, or kids needing extra focus
  • Customize your focus: striking, grappling, conditioning, or self-defense
  • Quiet, respectful setting with expert coaching

Sessions are held at our Garden City studio or may be arranged onsite by request. This is your time—train how you want, where you want, with full attention from your instructor.
